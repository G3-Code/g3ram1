My Story

Hi, I am Gayathri Ramakrishnan, a full stack web developer with immense experience in Project Management and Business Analysis. My IT journey started in 2000 when I joined Cognizant as a Programmer Analyst in 2000. By 2005, I was a team lead managing at least 7 team members and handling all the project's quality audits.

Between 2006 to 2016, I was trying to master the art of work-life balance. I took a variety of opportunities that helped me keep in touch with the industry with a primary focus on family and work flexibility.

In 2016, I joined LikeMinds as a Global Delivery - PM. I had the opportunity to work closely with the CEO & CTO of the company. I worked on resource strategizing, vendor management, requirements gathering, resource procurement, client interfacing, handling client escalations, RFP submissions and other project implementations across various geographic locations.

This journey of mine, moved me away from development. With the variety of experience I have had, I only felt I will enjoy development even more. I am very passionate about building apps that can make a difference in our lives. I am glad I was able to look back on my journey and choose to pursue the experience I like the best!
